    Draw a horizontal line through point \(A\text{.}\) Locate point \(B\text{,}\) \(\mm{75}\) to the right of \(A\text{.}\) Draw the vector from point \(A\) to point \(B\text{.}\)
  4. Add the arrowhead to indicate the direction.
